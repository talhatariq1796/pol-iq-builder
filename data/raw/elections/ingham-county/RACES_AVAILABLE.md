## Notes

### District Changes
State House and Congressional districts changed between elections due to redistricting:
- **2020**: U.S. House District 8, State House Districts 67, 68, 69
- **2022-2024**: U.S. House District 7, State House Districts 73, 74, 75, 77

### Precinct Coverage
All races have **complete precinct-level data** for Ingham County.

### Data Format
CSV files include vote breakdowns by:
- Total votes
- Election Day votes
- Absentee votes
- Early voting (2024 only)
- Mail-in votes
- Provisional votes
- Pre-processed absentee
- Absentee voter counting boards
